Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Fokus replaces your home screen with a focused, distraction-free environment designed around your goals — not the algorithm's.

🛡️

Focus Shield

Block specific features inside apps — YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, TikTok For You — without blocking the whole app.

⏱️

App Time Limits

Set daily limits per app. When the limit is reached, Focus Shield takes over. Extend with a one-time boost if needed.

📊

Usage Stats

Beautiful charts showing exactly where your time goes — by app, by day, by week. No guessing, no excuses.

❤️

Health Connect

Pair screen time with your health data — steps, sleep, heart rate, calories — all in one place, all on-device.

🤖

On-Device AI

Powered by Gemma 3 running entirely on your phone. Personalized summaries without anything leaving your device.

🔔

Notification Panel

View, dismiss, and snooze notifications from a clean in-launcher panel. Stay informed without being hijacked.
