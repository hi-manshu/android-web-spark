About

Built with intent.

Fokus started as a personal experiment — a launcher that actually helps you use your phone less, not more.

Most launchers compete to show you more — more widgets, more shortcuts, more notifications. Fokus does the opposite. It gets out of your way, enforces the boundaries you set, and gives you a clear picture of where your time actually goes.

The app is built with a strict set of principles: everything that can run on-device should run on-device. The AI summaries, the health data processing, the accessibility-powered blocking — none of it leaves your phone. Privacy isn't a feature. It's the starting point.
